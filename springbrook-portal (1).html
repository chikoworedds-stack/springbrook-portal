Our Values

The principles that guide how we show up for our patients and each other every day at Springbrook Dentistry.

Mission Statement To provide exceptional, compassionate dental care to the Alcoa community — making every patient feel valued, informed, and at ease. We build healthy smiles and lasting relationships, one appointment at a time.
Patient First
Every decision we make starts with what is best for the patient in our chair.
Excellence
We hold ourselves to the highest clinical and service standards, every appointment.
Integrity
We are honest with patients and with each other. Always.
Teamwork
We communicate openly, support one another, and celebrate wins together.
Growth
We are a learning practice. We welcome feedback and pursue continuous improvement.
Accountability
We own our work, follow through on commitments, and lift each other up.
