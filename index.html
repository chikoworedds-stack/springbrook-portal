Our Values

The principles that guide how we show up for our patients and each other every day at Springbrook Dentistry.

Mission Statement To provide exceptional, compassionate dental care to the Alcoa community — making every patient feel valued, informed, and at ease. We build healthy smiles and lasting relationships, one appointment at a time.
Integrity
We do what is right — for our patients, our team, and our practice — even when no one is watching.
Teamwork
We communicate openly, support one another, and celebrate wins together.
Professionalism
We hold ourselves to the highest standards in how we look, communicate, and perform every day.
Confidentiality
We protect our patients' privacy and handle all sensitive information with discretion and care.
Accountability
We own our work, follow through on commitments, and take responsibility for our actions.
Growth
We are a learning practice. We welcome feedback and pursue continuous improvement together.
